Privacy Policy for HumanLives

Introduction:

Thank you for using HumanLives . This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our barcode scanning feature.

Information We Collect:

When you use the barcode scanning feature in HumanLives, we may collect the following information:

  • Barcode data: When you scan a barcode, we collect the data associated with that barcode to provide you with information about the product.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, to improve the performance of our app.

How We Use Your Information:

We use the information collected through the barcode scanning feature for the following purposes:

  • Providing Product Information: We use barcode data to retrieve information about products and display it to you within the app.
  • Improving Our Services: We may analyze barcode scanning data to improve the accuracy and efficiency of our barcode scanning feature.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: We may use device information to identify and prevent fraudulent activities or misuse of our services.

Data Sharing:

We do not share the information collected through the barcode scanning feature with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you explicitly consent to such sharing.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the barcode scanning feature from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Updates to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website or through other appropriate means.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your information, please contact us at contact@humblebee.ai.

Effective Date:

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 01 Jun 2024 and applies to all users of the HumanLives Barcode Scanner app.
